// How it works

Three steps.
Under 60 seconds.

No setup, no account, no installation. Works in any browser, even offline.

1

Drop your file

Upload CSV, Excel, TSV, ODS or JSON. It loads into your browser using SQL.js — a real SQLite engine running as WebAssembly. Nothing is uploaded anywhere.

2

Ask in plain English

Type your question in English or German. Only the question — not your data — is sent to Gemini AI to generate SQL. Your spreadsheet stays on your device.

3

Get answer + chart

SQL runs locally in under a second. Results appear as a table with an AI insight, plus optional bar, line, pie or scatter chart with one click to switch.

🔒
Verify it yourself: Open DevTools → Network tab while running a query. You will not see your data in any outgoing request.
