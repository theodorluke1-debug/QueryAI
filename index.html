// How it works

Three steps.
Under 60 seconds.

No setup, no account, no installation. Works in any browser.

1

Drop your file

Upload a CSV or Excel file. It loads directly into your browser memory using SQL.js — a real SQLite engine. Nothing is uploaded anywhere.

2

Ask in plain English

Type your question. Only the question — not your data — is sent to Gemini to generate SQL. Your spreadsheet stays on your device.

3

Get your answer

The SQL runs locally in your browser. Results appear instantly with an AI insight explaining what they mean.

🔒
Privacy verified: Your file stays in RAM, not on a server. Open DevTools → Network while running a query — you won't see your data leave.
