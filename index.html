How it works

Three steps. Under 60 seconds.
100% private.

No setup, no account, no installation. Your data never leaves your device — guaranteed.

🔒 Privacy Verified: Your file stays in RAM, not on a server.
1
Drag & Drop — Local Processing
Drop your CSV or Excel file. It loads directly into your browser memory using SQL.js — a real SQLite database running locally. Nothing is uploaded anywhere.
2
Ask in Plain English
Type your question. Only the question (not your data) is sent to the AI to generate SQL. Your spreadsheet content stays on your device the entire time.
3
Get Charts Instantly
The SQL runs locally in your browser. Results appear as clear charts or numbers — with a smart insight on what it means. No waiting, no server round-trips.
